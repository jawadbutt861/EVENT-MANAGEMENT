## Updated Color Palette

### Primary Colors

**Rose** - `#FB7185`
- Usage: Primary buttons, active states, main CTAs
- Represents: Energy, passion, excitement
- Where: "Book Ticket" buttons, active nav links, primary actions

**Orange** - `#F97316`
- Usage: Secondary buttons, accents, highlights
- Represents: Enthusiasm, creativity, warmth
- Where: Secondary buttons, logout, cancel actions

**Success Green** - `#4ADE80`
- Usage: Success messages, valid tickets, confirmations
- Represents: Success, availability, positive actions
- Where: Valid ticket badges, success alerts

**Danger Red** - `#DC2626`
- Usage: Error messages, critical alerts
- Represents: Errors, warnings, critical states
- Where: Error messages, used tickets

**Warning Yellow** - `#FBBF24`
- Usage: Warning badges, "Sold Out", low ticket alerts
- Represents: Caution, urgency, attention
- Where: Sold out badges, low ticket warnings

### Supporting Colors

**Dark** - `#1F2933`
- Usage: Navbar, text, headers
- Creates: Professional, modern look

**Soft Peach** - `#FFF7ED`
- Usage: Page background
- Creates: Warm, inviting feel

**White** - `#FFFFFF`
- Usage: Cards, content areas
- Creates: Clean, modern look
